About Us

ELC, the brainchild of several luminaries and eminent personalities across the country and the globe, is a dream online platform which aims at proliferating knowledge and providing an ingenious understanding and cognizance of subjects to the school students, simultaneously aiming to generate diverse social, moral, educational and overall personality development to bring best out of budding mind and making sure that knowledge penetrates to every nook and corner to the growing students.

Mission

ELC has a team of more than “XYZ” staff from across the country to tackle basic problems which a student faces in day to day life. Putting forward a friendly environment to impart a proper Hassel free online education , it makes sure to encourage Students & Staff to connect efficiently and effectively.

Vision

Advancing knowledge by the profession teachers and is regarded central in providing access to online common platform to Teachers & Students by ensuring cooperative atmosphere of mutual respect to help each individual reach their optimum academic and social potential in a safe, respectful, and widely diverse learning community through a broad range of academics and extracurricular activities. To reward meritorious achievement and assist students in continuing their career brighter.
